RAS Empress: The World's First Luxury Air Liner.

Airships. Creaturekind is now well acquainted with the concept, yet nations are locked in fierce competition to propel their technologies forward, aiming for swifter, larger, and more reliable modes of conveyance. Advanced steam power, alternative fuels, and even the tantalizing prospect of nuclear fission dominate the scientific discourse. The world is increasingly interconnected, and the ambition to lead and profit from the burgeoning notion of long-distance travel has ignited a veritable race to the skies, both figuratively and literally.

Someone has finally crossed that finish line. Eastwyn, the crowning jewel of the Animal Kingdom, convened a press conference this past weekend to unveil a newly patented and enigmatic design: The Infinite Current.

Accompanying this startling announcement is the revelation that they have clandestinely implemented this design for governmental purposes with notable success. To present this innovation to the public, they have crafted something surpassing the traditional airship, christened "RAS Empress."
