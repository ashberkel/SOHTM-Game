RAS Empress: The World's First Luxury Air Liner

Airships. Creaturekind is now well acquainted with the concept, yet nations have been locked in fierce competition to propel the technology forward; The aim for swifter, larger, and more reliable modes of conveyance is not just in the minds of fantasies anymore. Advanced steam power, alternative fuels, and even the tantalizing prospect of nuclear fission dominate the scientific discourse.

The world is increasingly interconnected, and the ambition to lead and profit from the burgeoning notion of long-distance travel has ignited a veritable race to the skies, both figuratively and literally.

Someone has finally crossed the finish line. Eastwyn, the crowning jewel of the Animal Kingdom, convened a press conference this past weekend to unveil a newly patented and enigmatic design:

The Infinite Current. Accompanying this announcement is the revelation that they have clandestinely implemented this design for governmental purposes with notable success. This suggests that there is much more to their research than presented surface level.
